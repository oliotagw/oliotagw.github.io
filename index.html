Context

My mother's people immigrated from Latvia to the US in the 1880s. My father's people have lived in Yucatán for millenia and immigrated from Spain within the last few centuries. Since the 2010s, I have been living in Tiwa (Pueblo), and Gulgahén, Saidindê, and Shis-Inday (Apache) regions.
